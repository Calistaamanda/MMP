About Us

MUTIARA MAHAKAM PERSADA

Leading coal mining and trading company PT. MUTIARA MAHAKAM PERSADA is dedicated to excellence in the sector. We have made a name for ourselves in the coal industry with years of experience and commitment. We stand out in the industry thanks to our emphasis on sustainability, dependability, and quality. As we uphold the highest standards of environmental responsibility and safety, we reliably provide value to our clients through strategic collaborations and creative techniques. Our mission at PT. MUTIARA MAHAKAM PERSADA is to address the world's energy demands as a reliable partner while making a good impact on the communities and surroundings in which we operate.

Vision

To be a top business in the coal mining sector, recognized for our dedication to innovation, sustainability, and quality.

Mission

To continuously and dependablely supply our clients with coal of the highest caliber.

To put our workers' health and safety first and to uphold morality and justice in the workplace.

To forge solid alliances in order to promote sustainability and mutual growth with clients, vendors, and local communities.

To make research and development investments in order to grow our company internationally and continuously enhance operational efficiency.

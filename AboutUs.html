PT. MUTIARA MAHAKAM PERSADA is a coal mining trading company headquartered in East Kalimantan, Samarinda, Indonesia. Established since 2016, the company operates across all sectors of the coal mining industry, actively involved in exploration, production, transportation, and sales.

Service

Coal Delivery
PT. MUTIARA MAHAKAM PERSADA provides efficient and reliable coal delivery services. Our services encompass the transportation of coal from the mines to the ports, as well as delivery from ports to destinations worldwide. We ensure timely delivery, adhering to high safety and quality standards.
Mining Logistics
We also offer comprehensive mining logistics services. This includes mine supply chain management, coal storage, as well as internal distribution to ensure smooth production and delivery processes.
Mining Consultation
PT. MUTIARA MAHAKAM PERSADA offers mining consultation services to our clients. Our team of experts is ready to provide the best advice and solutions in mine management, production efficiency enhancement, as well as compliance with industry regulations and standards.
Coal Quality Monitoring
We provide coal quality monitoring services to ensure compliance with customer-specified specifications. Our team conducts regular testing and analysis to ensure the coal quality is maintained throughout the processing and delivery process.
Inventory Management
PT. MUTIARA MAHAKAM PERSADA also provides inventory management services to our clients. We assist in organizing and monitoring coal stocks to ensure sufficient availability and prevent supply shortages.
